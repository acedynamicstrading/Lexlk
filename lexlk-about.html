How It Works

From Village to Verdict

JusticeLK makes it possible for any citizen, anywhere in Sri Lanka, to access the legal system.

1
Field Officer Registers
A trained village officer logs in on a mobile phone or computer
2
7-Step Wizard
Client details, case type, facts, and documents captured through a guided flow
3
AI Analysis
Claude AI generates a case summary and court petition in seconds
4
Legal Aid Routing
System identifies the correct provincial court and legal route for the case type
5
Token Issued
Client receives a printable token (GU-YEAR-XXXXXX) to track their case

About the Project

Proposed to the Chief Justice

⚖️
"A judicial endorsement is the critical first step — it unlocks the government partnership, which in turn enables UNICEF and Save the Children to formally engage."
— LexLK Proposal to the Hon. Chief Justice of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, March 2026

LexLK was built to solve a structural gap in Sri Lanka's justice system: rural citizens have no practical pathway to formal legal representation. The distance to provincial courts, the cost of legal advice, and language barriers collectively exclude millions from access to justice.

The platform is fully operational today on GitHub Pages, powered by Google Sheets, Google Drive, and Anthropic's Claude AI. It requires zero government server infrastructure at the prototype stage.

The proposal to the Chief Justice outlines a 3-year government migration plan to ICTA servers, integration with the Legal Aid Commission, and a national schools outreach programme reaching all 25 districts.
